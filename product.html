Bottle-top Dispensers Dispensette® S,
analog-adjustable, DE-M

High-precision, chemically resistant bottle-top dispenser with analog volume adjustment and ATEX certification

  • Simply adjustable to any volume by analog setting – with safe-lock function
  • With efficient, adjustable intake tube dispenser
  • Precisely calibrated – satisfies highest requirements of accuracy and precision
  • Available with or without recirculation valve
  • ATEX certification for use in potentially explosive environments
  • Autoclavable at 121 °C / 15 min (PTFE/glass version)

Dispensette® S is the standard bottle-top dispenser that dispenses only pure, non-acidic media. With its analogue adjustable volume setting and high-quality materials (FEP or glass/PTFE), it offers laboratory-grade precision for daily dispensing routines. The DE-M marking confirms metrological verification according to German calibration regulations.
