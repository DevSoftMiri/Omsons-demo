The OMSONS Difference

Why OMSONS?

For over four decades, OMSONS has been at the forefront of scientific instrument manufacturing — engineering precision tools that empower researchers, clinicians, and industry professionals to achieve results they can trust.

🔬

ISO-Certified Precision Manufacturing

Every instrument leaves our facility having passed rigorous metrological validation. Our in-house calibration laboratory is accredited to international standards.

🧬

Purpose-Built for Life Sciences

From volumetric glassware to liquid handling systems, our product range is engineered specifically for biological, pharmaceutical, and analytical workflows.

⚗️

Traceable to SI Units

All OMSONS volumetric instruments carry full traceability documentation linked to national measurement standards — a non-negotiable requirement for GLP, GMP, and ISO 17025-compliant laboratories.

🛡️

Uncompromising Material Quality

We use only pharmaceutical-grade borosilicate glass, chemical-resistant polymers, and inert metal alloys — selected for their compatibility with the most demanding reagents.

🌍

Global Reach, Local Support

Through our international distribution network spanning 40+ countries, OMSONS provides responsive technical support and rapid spare-part delivery.

📊

Data Integrity by Design

Our instruments are designed with audit trails, error-detection mechanisms, and digital connectivity — helping your laboratory maintain full data integrity.
